About Us

We started Cravespresso with a passion for quality coffee and community connection.

Our Story:

1. When was the establishment created and how did the idea come about?

The idea started when my partner and I often found ourselves craving a cozy place to relax somewhere different from the usual spots, where we could enjoy a good cup of coffee and just unwind. That craving sparked the concept of creating our own space that offers that kind of vibe. Eventually, we turned that idea into reality.

2. How many people are working with you?

Currently, we're a team of four dedicated members. Each of us plays a vital role in keeping everything running smoothly, from preparing the drinks to ensuring our customers always feel welcome and satisfied.

3. Why did you choose a truck for your establishment?

We chose to go with a coffee truck because it's something unique, cost-efficient, and flexible. Unlike a traditional café, it allows us to be mobile and bring our coffee experience to different places. It also helped us start with lower overhead costs while still offering a standout experience.

4. How is your establishment doing so far? Would you consider it successful?

Absolutely. In just five months, we've seen incredible support from the community. We've received a lot of positive feedback and have already built a solid base of loyal customers. It's been a rewarding journey so far, and we're proud of what we've achieved.

5. What do you think about the future of your establishment?

We're very excited about the future. One of our main goals is to eventually open a standalone café with a nature-inspired design — a more permanent space where people can relax, stay longer, and enjoy a wider variety of snacks and meals. At the same time, we plan to keep our original coffee truck in its current location to continue serving our regular customers.
